Tacocat

Tacocat is a coding challenge based on the more traditional String-Reversal exercise, but with a twist! It determines if the word or phrase entered is a palindrome.

Try the App!

String reversal is an exercise often used in some form by technology companies such as and Amazon as an impromptu algorithm challenge for job candidates.
